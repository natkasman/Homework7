What is Voltage?
Energy, stored in the form of an electric charge capable of provoking electrons to flow through a conductor.

What is Resistance?
Opposition to electric current (dependent on the material, cross-sectional area, and temperature).

Is a short circuit an example of a closed or open circuit?
Closed.

How can you determine if a cricuit is closed or open?
A closed circuit allows current to flow, but an open circuit does not.
